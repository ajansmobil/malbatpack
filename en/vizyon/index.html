Our Vision - To Become the Trust Brand of the Industry

As Malbat Pack, our vision is to be a globally recognized leading company in the packaging industry, preferred for quality and reliability. With our customer-oriented approach and continuous development approach, we aim to reach a brand position that sets standards in the sector.

Leadership and Pioneering

We aim to be the most reliable supplier in Turkey and the region in the packaging industry. With our innovative products, superior quality standards and excellent customer service, we want to strengthen our pioneering position in the sector. In testliner, kraftliner, fluting paper and specialty paper products, we aim to be a company that provides solutions to all kinds of needs of our customers from a single point by offering the widest product range.

Global Growth

While maintaining our strong position in the local market, we want to increase our presence in international markets. We aim to become a global brand by exporting to European, Middle Eastern and African countries. By understanding the cultural and technical needs of our customers in different countries, we want to produce special solutions for them. By expanding our strategic partnerships and distributor network, we aim to become a world-renowned packaging supplier.

Technological Superiority

We aim to increase efficiency through automation and digitalization by equipping our production facilities with the latest technology machines. Establishing smart factory systems in accordance with Industry 4.0 principles, developing artificial intelligence-supported quality control processes and optimizing our production processes with IoT technologies are part of our vision. We plan to establish an R&D center and develop next-generation packaging solutions.

Sustainability Leadership

We want to lead the sector in environmentally friendly production methods. We plan to establish carbon-neutral production facilities, switch to 100% renewable energy use and reach zero waste target by 2030. We aim to be a role model company for a sustainable future by developing packaging solutions that are fully compatible with the circular economy model and have maximum recycling rate.

Investing in People

By investing in the development of our employees, we aim to be the company with the most qualified workforce in the sector. We want to keep employee satisfaction at the highest level with continuous training programs, career planning support and fair wage policy. We plan to establish university collaborations and develop internship programs to bring young talents to the sector. We act with the understanding that happy employees mean satisfied customers.
