Our Mission - Quality Packaging for a Sustainable Future

As Malbat Pack, our mission is to lead the sector with environmentally friendly and sustainable packaging solutions, to add value to our customers by offering high quality products. With our technology and innovation-oriented approach, we produce solutions that shape the future of the packaging industry.

Quality and Excellence

We aim for the highest quality standards in every product we produce. Within the framework of ISO 9001 quality management system, we apply strict controls at every stage of our production processes. We produce in accordance with international standards in testliner, kraftliner, fluting paper and all our other products. Delivering flawless products to our customers is an indispensable principle for us.

Environment and Sustainability

Producing environmentally friendly for a sustainable future is at the center of our mission. By using recyclable raw materials, we contribute to protecting natural resources and reducing waste amount. With our FSC and PEFC certified raw materials, we support responsible forest management. With our energy efficiency programs, we try to minimize energy consumption in our production processes. We invest in renewable energy sources to reduce our carbon footprint.

Customer Focus

The success of our customers is our success. By producing special solutions for each customer, we offer the most suitable designs for their packaging needs. We aim to keep customer satisfaction at the highest level with fast production, timely delivery and flexible payment terms. With our after-sales support services, we continue to be with our customers.

Innovation and Technology

We closely follow the developments in packaging technologies and integrate the latest innovations into our production processes. With our R&D studies, we develop lighter, more durable and more economical packaging solutions. With digital printing technologies, special coating methods and new generation paper types, we offer products that provide competitive advantage to our customers.

Social Responsibility

We are aware of our responsibilities towards the society and environment in which we operate. Providing a safe and healthy working environment for our employees, contributing to local employment and supporting social projects are the cornerstones of our social responsibility approach. We continue to do business in a transparent and honest manner without compromising our ethical values.
