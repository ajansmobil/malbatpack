Coated White Top Kraftliner - The Pinnacle of Packaging Excellence

Malbat Pack coated white top kraftliner is the most advanced product of packaging technology. The special white coating applied on the strong base produced from virgin kraft cellulose provides both maximum strength and excellent print quality. This premium paper is used in packaging of luxury cosmetics, perfume, electronics, gourmet food, and top-segment products. This product, which combines the legendary durability of kraft with the bright whiteness of coated white testliner, adds both strength and prestige to your brand.

Advanced Coating Technology

In coated white top kraftliner production, multi-layer white coating is applied on virgin kraft cellulose base. The coating layer consists of pigment, binder, and special additives. Whiteness degree is between 85-92% according to ISO 2470 standard, which provides crystal clear printing possibility. Surface smoothness (Bendtsen method) is below 60 ml/min, ideal for photo-quality printing. Gloss value can be adjusted between 50-70 GU. Coating thickness is precisely controlled between 15-25 microns. Opacity is over 95%, which prevents the base layer from reflecting on the print.

Maximum Strength Properties

The virgin kraft layer on the back surface gives the product extraordinary strength. Our grammage options are offered in a wide range between 175 GSM and 450 GSM. Ring Crush Test (RCT) values are at the same level as standard kraftliner. Burst strength is minimum 500 kPa. Tensile strength is high in both MD (Machine Direction) and CD (Cross Direction). ECT (Edge Crush Test) results show high stacking capacity of boxes. Bending stiffness is high, boxes maintain shape stability.

Professional Print Excellence

Coated white top kraftliner shows flawless compatibility with all advanced printing technologies. Resolution up to 133 lpi is achieved with HD flexo printing (high definition flexo printing). Photo-realistic images can be printed in sheet-fed offset printing. Excellent toner or ink transfer is provided in digital printing (HP Indigo, Xerox). Wide color gamut is achieved with 6-color process printing. Full tone matching is achieved in Pantone and HKS special colors. Shine and effect are at maximum level in metallic inks. Fast drying and vibrant colors are achieved in UV inks. It is perfectly compatible with varnish, UV varnish, dispersion varnish applications.

Special Finishing Processes

Coated white top kraftliner is suitable for all premium finishing techniques. Gold, silver, and holographic foils show excellent adhesion in hot stamping applications. Bright metallic effects are achieved in cold foil technique. Deep and clear embossing can be made in embossing process. Elegant and sophisticated appearance is provided with debossing. 3D effect is given to selected areas with spot UV varnish. High-contrast designs are created with matt+gloss combinations. Velvet feel is provided with soft-touch lamination. Structural integrity is maintained in window patching operations.

Premium Application Areas

Coated white top kraftliner has become standard in luxury cosmetic brand packaging. It provides both aesthetics and protection in premium perfume boxes. It makes the unboxing experience unforgettable in high-end electronic products (smart watch, premium headphone, tablet). It is used in the premium series of gourmet chocolate and special tea-coffee brands. It creates a luxury perception in jewelry and watch boxes. It emphasizes brand value in designer shoe and accessory boxes. It expresses prestige in corporate gift and promotional boxes. It stands out in packaging of special series and limited edition products.

Malbat Pack Ultra Premium Service

In Malbat Pack coated white top kraftliner production, pharmaceutical grade quality controls are applied. More than 25 tests are performed for each production batch and a detailed QC report is prepared. Color consistency is measured with spectrophotometer. Surface density control is performed with densitometer. Inline quality control systems are available on each roll. Special color formulations are developed and standardization is provided with Color Management System (CMS). Error risk is reduced to zero with pre-production approval process.
