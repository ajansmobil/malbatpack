White Top Kraftliner - Perfect Balance of Strength and Aesthetics

Malbat Pack white top kraftliner is a hybrid paper produced from virgin kraft cellulose with white top surface and natural kraft back surface. Thanks to this special structure, both the high strength of kraft and the aesthetic appearance of white surface are utilized. This paper with high print quality and maximum durability is an ideal packaging solution for premium segment products. It is a first-class material preferred in export boxes, food packaging, and every application where brand value needs to be highlighted.

Double-Sided Advantage

In white top kraftliner production, a white fiber layer is coated on the virgin kraft cellulose base. Top surface whiteness degree is between 70-80% according to ISO 2470 standard, which provides excellent print quality. Back surface is natural kraft color and maintains high strength properties. Our grammage options range from 150 GSM to 400 GSM. This grammage range provides usage flexibility in medium and heavy load transportation applications. Ring Crush Test (RCT) values are at the same level as virgin kraftliner. Burst resistance is high and safely protects your products during long-distance transportation.

Wide Usage Spectrum

White top kraftliner is widely used in the food sector, especially in pizza boxes, cake boxes, ready meal packaging, and export food boxes. In cosmetics and personal care product packaging, aesthetics and durability are offered together. It provides an appearance that strengthens brand image in e-commerce premium products. It is preferred in textile products, especially shirt boxes and shoe boxes. In electronics product packaging, it offers both protection and elegant appearance. It provides hygienic white surface advantage in pharmaceutical and health product packaging. It creates a premium perception in gift boxes and custom-designed boxes.

Superior Print Surface

White top surface shows perfect compatibility with all printing methods. Sharp edges and clear details are obtained in flexo printing. Raster dots come out flawlessly in offset printing and color transitions are smooth. Toner or ink transfer is optimal in digital printing. Vibrant colors are obtained in 4-color (CMYK) prints. The most realistic tones are captured in Pantone color applications. Details such as logos, barcodes, QR codes are printed in crystal clarity. Surface protection and shiny appearance are provided with UV varnish application. A natural and sophisticated feel is obtained with matt varnish. Selected areas can be emphasized with varnish application.

Performance and Durability

White top kraftliner exhibits superior strength thanks to the virgin kraft layer on its back surface. Vertical load carrying capacity is high and stacking performance is excellent. Moisture resistance is better than testliner papers. Tear resistance is high and the risk of damage during filling and transportation is minimal. Thanks to its shock absorption properties, your products are protected from shipping shocks. Corner strength is high and box structure remains intact for a long time. It has a stable structure against temperature and humidity changes.

Malbat Pack Hybrid Solution

In Malbat Pack white top kraftliner production, we use the most advanced technologies. In our multi-layer production process, each layer is controlled separately and optimum adhesion is ensured. Bonding strength tests between white layer and kraft base are performed regularly. Risk of delamination (layer separation) is zero. Technical data sheet is prepared for each production batch. Special grammage and whiteness combinations can be produced according to customer needs. We offer the opportunity to test the product with fast sample service. Create both strong and elegant packaging with white top kraftliner.
