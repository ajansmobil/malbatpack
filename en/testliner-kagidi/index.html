Testliner Paper - Economical and Eco-Friendly Solution

Malbat Pack testliner paper is an economical packaging material produced from recycled paper waste and used on the outer surface of corrugated boxes. Our testliner paper, which is a reflection of our sustainable production approach, both protects your budget and enables you to make the right choice for the environment. Our grammage range varies between 90 GSM and 200 GSM, and we offer solutions suitable for different application areas.

Production Process and Technical Properties

In our testliner paper production, recycled cardboard and paper waste are used as raw materials. In our modern production lines, these wastes are converted into high-quality paper through special processes. Our production process is carried out in accordance with ISO 9001 and environmental management systems standards. The surface strength (Cobb test) and burst resistance (Burst test) values of our testliner paper meet international standards. With our double-sided testliner option, you can achieve quality appearance on both top and bottom surfaces.

Wide Range of Applications

Testliner paper is widely used in e-commerce boxes, shipping packaging, archive boxes, pizza boxes, shoe boxes, and general-purpose packaging applications. Ideal for medium-weight loads, testliner is the smartest choice in terms of cost-performance balance. Thanks to its flat surface, it can be easily printed with flexo, offset, and digital printing methods. Thanks to our food safety certificates, you can safely use it in food contact packaging. It is preferred as outer surface paper in 3-ply, 5-ply, and 7-ply cardboard production.

Malbat Pack Quality and Service

As Malbat Pack, we prioritize quality in our testliner paper production. Grammage homogeneity, moisture content, strength tests, and color stability controls are performed in each production batch. Our experienced sales team helps you determine the most suitable testliner grammage and type for your needs. Thanks to our stock depth, we can deliver quickly. We offer secure shipping options throughout Turkey. Special price advantages and flexible payment terms are available for large batches. We provide stock management and logistics support to our long-term business partners.

Environmental Responsibility and Sustainability

Testliner paper is one of the best examples of the circular economy. Reintroducing used paper into the economy contributes both to the conservation of natural resources and to the reduction of waste amount. In Malbat Pack testliner production, we use technologies that increase energy efficiency and minimize water consumption. Wastewater from our facilities is discharged in a way that does not harm the environment by passing through advanced treatment systems. By using recycled testliner, you can reduce your company's carbon footprint and create a sustainable brand image. We provide our customers with the necessary documents for environmental reporting.
