Kraftliner Paper - Premium Quality Kraft Solution

Malbat Pack kraftliner paper is a premium quality paper produced from 100% virgin cellulose and used on the outer surface of corrugated boxes. With its natural brown color and high strength properties, it offers both an aesthetic and functional packaging solution. Kraftliner, especially preferred in export packaging, heavy load transportation, long-distance shipping, and challenging storage conditions, guarantees top-level performance. It is ideal for companies that want to create an organic and eco-friendly brand image with its natural appearance.

Virgin Cellulose and Superior Quality

In our kraftliner production, virgin cellulose obtained from long-fiber coniferous trees (especially Scandinavian pine) is used. Not containing recycled fiber provides maximum strength and long life. Our grammage options are offered in a wide range between 125 GSM and 440 GSM. It shows high performance in burst strength test and SCT (Short span Compression Test) values are above industry standards. Ring Crush Test (RCT) results show that the stacking capacity of boxes is high. Water absorption is minimized by keeping the Cobb value under control.

Versatile Application Areas

Kraftliner paper is the first-class choice for export boxes and meets international shipping standards. It provides high protection in packaging of automotive spare parts, machine parts, and metal products. In the food industry, it is especially used in dry food, fruit-vegetable export, and canned goods boxes. It offers a safe solution in packaging of powder and granule products in the chemical sector. It is ideal for safe delivery of premium products in e-commerce logistics. It is used as outer surface paper in 5-ply and 7-ply cardboard production. It is an indispensable material in packaging of heavy industrial products. The aesthetic appearance of natural kraft is utilized in custom-designed boxes.

Printing and Finishing Processes

The natural brown surface of kraftliner paper is suitable for flexo and offset printing. Clear results are obtained in single-color or multi-color prints. High-contrast designs can be created with white ink. Surface protection is provided by preserving the natural kraft appearance with matt varnish application. Shine can be added with varnish application. Environmentally friendly printing can be done using water-based inks. Logos and text can be applied as embossing with embossing and debossing processes. Non-printed use is also possible when natural appearance is preferred.

Environmental Advantages

Virgin kraftliner is produced with raw materials from sustainable forestry practices. Our FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) and PEFC (Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification) certificates guarantee responsible forest management. It does not harm nature with its 100% biodegradable and compostable feature. The kraft process optimizes energy and water consumption by providing high efficiency. Use of renewable energy is being increased in our production facilities and carbon-neutral production is targeted. Bleaching chemicals are not needed thanks to its natural color.

Malbat Pack Excellence Standard

In Malbat Pack kraftliner production, rigorous quality controls are applied at every stage. Fiber analysis, strength tests, grammage homogeneity, and color stability are measured regularly. Technical data sheets (TDS) and test certificates are provided to our customers. Custom production can be done for special grammage and strength requirements. We offer inventory management and just-in-time delivery services. We determine the optimum design of your box with our packaging engineering support. Add strength and quality to your brand with kraftliner.
