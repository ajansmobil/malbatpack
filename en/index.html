Get to Know Malbat Pack
With over 20 years of experience as Iraq's largest box and packaging manufacturer, we offer customized packaging solutions for every need through our advanced technology and modern production facilities. With our wide product range for food, cosmetics, pharmaceutical and industrial sectors, high quality standards and customer satisfaction-oriented approach, we are the leading company in the sector. We are committed to providing the best service to our customers with our production capacity, experienced team and R&D studies.
Our Vision
To be the most reliable and preferred brand in the Middle East in the packaging sector, and to reach a leading position in the global market with sustainable production and innovative solutions.
Our Mission
To add value to the sector with modern technology and our expert staff by providing high quality, environmentally friendly and economical packaging solutions to our customers, to prioritize continuous improvement and customer satisfaction.
