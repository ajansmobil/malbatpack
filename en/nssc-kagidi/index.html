NSSC Paper - Semi-Chemical Sulfate Technology

Malbat Pack NSSC (Neutral Semi-Chemical Sulfite) paper is a premium quality raw material produced by the semi-chemical sulfate method and used in corrugated board production. Thanks to this special production method, both high mechanical strength and superior flexibility properties are offered together. NSSC paper is a first-class paper type preferred especially in industrial packaging requiring heavy load transportation.

Production Technology and Properties

In NSSC paper production, cellulose fibers are obtained by treating wood chips with semi-chemical process. This method provides high strength by largely preserving the natural structure of the fibers. Our grammage range varies between 112-140 GSM, and different grammage options can also be produced according to your special requests. The most important feature of NSSC paper is that it has a high Ring Crush Test (RCT) value. This significantly increases the vertical load carrying capacity of boxes.

Application Areas and Advantages

NSSC paper is widely used in heavy machine parts, automotive spare parts, white goods, construction materials, and export packaging. Thanks to its high strength-to-weight ratio, you can transport more load with lighter boxes. Since its moisture resistance is high, it maintains its performance even in humid environments. Shock absorption is at an excellent level, safely protecting your products even during long-distance transportation. Additionally, it supports your brand's professional appearance by offering first-class printing quality.

Malbat Pack Production Standard

As Malbat Pack, we comply with international quality standards in our NSSC paper production. In our modern production facilities, detailed laboratory tests are performed for each batch and results are shared with our customers. Tests such as burst test, RCT test, moisture analysis, and grammage control are routinely performed. With our technical consultancy service, we help you determine the most suitable NSSC grammage for your packaging project. Thanks to our fast production and delivery processes, we meet your needs without disrupting your business planning.

Sustainability and Economy

In our NSSC paper production, we pay attention to efficient resource use and maximize the recycling rate. The semi-chemical production process requires less energy consumption compared to full chemical methods and minimizes environmental impacts. At the same time, raw material use is optimized thanks to the high yield rate. We establish long-term business partnerships by offering our customers both high quality and competitive price advantages. Our NSSC paper is a smart packaging solution that reduces your costs without compromising performance.
