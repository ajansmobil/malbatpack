Coated White Testliner - The Pinnacle of Luxury Packaging

Malbat Pack coated white testliner is a premium quality packaging material designed for top-segment products. Its surface treated with special coating technology offers unique whiteness and smoothness, reflecting your brand's prestige at the highest level. This paper, preferred in luxury cosmetics, perfume, jewelry, electronics, and gourmet food packaging, enables you to convey the value of the product you offer to your customers through packaging.

Advanced Coating Technology

In our coated white testliner production, a multi-layer coating system is used. The special coating applied to the surface consists of pigments, binders, and additives. Thanks to this coating, the whiteness degree exceeds 90% and reaches the highest level in ISO 2470 standard. Surface smoothness is below 50 ml/min according to the Bendtsen method, which provides a mirror-like flat printing surface. Our grammage options range from 140 GSM to 350 GSM. Coating thickness is controlled between 15-25 microns, which provides optimum print performance.

Superior Print Performance

Coated white testliner shows perfect compatibility with all advanced printing techniques. In offset printing, ink absorption is controlled and raster dots come out sharp and clear. In flexo printing, ink spread is at minimum level. In digital printing, toner or ink transfer is optimal and color vibrancy is at maximum level. In 4-color (CMYK), 6-color, and Pantone color prints, the most realistic color tones are obtained. It gives flawless results in special printing techniques such as hot stamping, cold foil, embossing, and debossing. It is perfectly compatible with UV spot varnish, dispersion varnish, and varnish applications.

Premium Product Packaging

Coated white testliner is especially used in packaging of high value-added products. This paper, ideal for perfume and cosmetic boxes, supports the luxury perception of the product. It is perfect for minimal and elegant designs in electronic products (smartphone, tablet, headphone). It is frequently preferred in packaging of premium food products such as gourmet chocolate, special tea, and coffee. It is indispensable in jewelry boxes, watch boxes, and luxury accessory packaging. It makes the unboxing experience unforgettable for e-commerce premium segment products. It best represents your corporate image in corporate gift boxes.

Malbat Pack Premium Service

As Malbat Pack, we apply the highest level of quality controls in coated white testliner production. Whiteness test (ISO 2470), smoothness test (Bendtsen), gloss test (ISO 8254), opacity test, and color stability control are performed in each production batch. We offer custom sample production to our customers, providing the opportunity to test your design on real paper. With our pre-press consultancy service, we make technical recommendations for your packaging design to come out best. With fast prototype production, you can bring your ideas to life in a short time. Stand out from your competitors with coated white testliner that adds value to your brand.
