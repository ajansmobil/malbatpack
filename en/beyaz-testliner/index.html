White Testliner - Premium Appearance and Print Quality

Malbat Pack white testliner is an ideal product for companies seeking premium packaging solutions with its high whiteness degree and superior print quality. White testliner maintains all durability features of standard testliner paper while giving your brand a more elegant and professional appearance thanks to its white surface. It is an indispensable packaging material especially for companies that want to highlight brand value.

Technical Properties and Whiteness Standards

In our white testliner production, carefully selected white paper waste and virgin cellulose mixture are used. Our whiteness degree ranges from 75-85% according to ISO 2470 standard. Our grammage options are between 110 GSM and 250 GSM. Our surface smoothness (Bendtsen) value is high, which provides excellent print quality. By keeping the Cobb value (water absorption) under control, ink spread in offset and flexo printing is minimized. Strength properties are at the same level as standard testliner and can be safely used in applications requiring heavy load transportation.

Application Areas and Sector Preferences

White testliner is widely used in packaging of high value-added products such as cosmetic boxes, pharmaceutical packaging, electronic product boxes, food packaging, textile boxes, gift boxes, and e-commerce premium boxes. This paper, preferred in every sector where brand perception is important, allows you to reflect the quality of the product you offer to your customers with packaging. Color vibrancy in colored prints is excellent and captures true color tones in CMYK prints. It is ideal for printing details such as QR codes and barcodes clearly.

Print Performance and Design Flexibility

White testliner is compatible with all offset, flexo, digital and screen printing methods. Thanks to its high whiteness degree, maximum color vibrancy is achieved with minimum ink usage, which reduces your printing costs. It has a suitable structure for UV varnish, dispersion varnish and lamination applications. Matt or glossy surface options are available. It gives excellent results in special finishing processes such as emboss, deboss and hot foil. White testliner, which does not limit the imagination of your graphic designers, enables you to tell your brand's story in the best way.
