Malbat Pack - The Trust Brand of Packaging Industry

As Malbat Pack, we are a quality and reliability-focused company operating in the paper and cardboard packaging industry. With years of experience and sectoral knowledge, we offer the best packaging solutions to our customers. With our team specialized in corrugated boxes, paper packaging and custom design packaging products, we serve domestic and foreign customers.

Experience and Expertise

With many years of experience gained in the sector, we produce products according to international standards using our latest technology machines in our modern production facilities. With a wide range of products such as testliner, kraftliner, fluting paper, NSSC and white top papers, we provide solutions for all your packaging needs. Thanks to our quality control processes, every stage of our products is meticulously inspected and flawless products are delivered to our customers.

Our Values and Principles

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. By producing special solutions for each customer, we offer the most suitable packaging designs for their needs. Our team, working with the principles of honesty, transparency and professionalism, aims to establish long-term business partnerships with our customers. Thanks to our innovation and continuous development approach, we closely follow the innovations in the sector and offer the most up-to-date solutions to our customers.

Sustainability and Environmental Awareness

As Malbat Pack, we adopt environmentally friendly production methods. Using recyclable raw materials, we produce nature-friendly packaging solutions. With our raw material supply with FSC and PEFC certificates, we support sustainable forestry practices. With our energy efficiency-focused production processes, we try to minimize our carbon footprint. We are aware of our environmental responsibilities to leave a livable world to future generations.

Our Service Approach

The service we provide to our customers is not limited to product delivery only. With our value-added services such as packaging consultancy, custom design support, fast production and logistics solutions, we are with our business partners. Our technical team helps you determine the most suitable paper grammage, box design and printing techniques for your packaging projects. Serving with quality, speed and reliability are our most basic principles.
