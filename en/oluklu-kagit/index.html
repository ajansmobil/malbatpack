Fluting Paper - The Cornerstone of Corrugated Board Production

Malbat Pack fluting paper is a high-quality packaging material that forms the inner layer of corrugated boxes and provides maximum protection for your products. Thanks to its corrugated structure between two flat paper layers, it provides flexibility and durability to the box. Our fluting papers produced with modern production technology are offered to you in accordance with both domestic and international standards.

Technical Properties of Fluting Paper

In our fluting paper production, grammage options range from 80 GSM to 200 GSM. This wide range allows us to provide suitable solutions for all your packaging needs. Flute types include A, B, C, E, and F flute options. A flute is ideal for heavy loads requiring high strength, while E and F flutes are perfect for thin and elegant boxes. Our products are manufactured from 100% recyclable raw materials, making them an excellent choice for companies seeking environmentally friendly packaging solutions.

Application Areas and Advantages

Fluting paper is used in a wide range of applications including e-commerce boxes, industrial packaging, food boxes, electronic product packaging, and shipping boxes. Its corrugated structure absorbs impacts and protects your products during transportation. While its lightweight nature reduces transportation costs, its high strength properties enable you to safely transport heavy loads. Moreover, thanks to its excellent printing surface, you can produce custom-designed boxes that best represent your brand.
